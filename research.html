Research Interests

My research is in Commutative Algebra and its interactions with Algebraic Geometry and Combinatorics. Some keywords are: positive characteristic techniques, invariant theory, determinantal varieties, differential operators, linkage and residual intersections, local cohomology modules, symbolic powers.
