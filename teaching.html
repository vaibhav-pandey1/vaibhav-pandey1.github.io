Teaching Outreach

  1. Teaching assistant for Pre-REU in Dynamical Systems at the University of Utah in Summer 2022, organized by Prof. Kurt Vinhage. The aim was to introduce discrete dynamical systems on the unit interval in a collaborative mathematical environment with emphasis on proof writing.
  2. Co-organized the Graduate Teacher Training Program in Fall 2021 at the University of Utah. This week-long program trains incoming graduate students to improve their teaching and lab instruction skills, and to understand the department culture.
  3. Co-organized the Summer High School Program at the University of Utah in Summer 2019 with Prof. Stefan Patrikis. The aim was to introduce Number Theory to high school students.
