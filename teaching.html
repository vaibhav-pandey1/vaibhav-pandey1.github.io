1. Teaching assistant for Pre-REU in Dynamical Systems held at the University of Utah in Summer 2022 organized by Prof. Kurt Vinhage . The aim was to introduce discrete dynamical systems on the unit interval in a collaborative mathematical envirnoment with emphesis on proof writing.

3. Co-organized the Summer High School Program held at the University of Utah in Summer 2019 with Prof. Stefan Patrikis . The aim was to introduce Number Theory to high school students.
